About

Built by a founder
who needed this

OutreachOS was born from pure frustration. Vigasini Kamalanathan — Vic — was managing 200+ LinkedIn contacts across internship applications, consulting leads, and investor conversations in a messy spreadsheet. She missed follow-ups. She forgot context. She sent the same generic message twice to the same person.

So she built the tool she wished existed: one dashboard that tracks the full pipeline, tells you what to do next, and drafts messages that actually sound human — powered by Groq AI and built on a dead-simple playbook system.

Today OutreachOS is used by SDRs, founders, BD leads, and job hunters who treat their pipeline like a serious asset.
